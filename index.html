Our Mission

Kalikamai Youth Society empowers disenfranchised communities in Nepal to achieve sustainable development and social justice by implementing collaborative, community-led solutions for positive transformation.

Our Impact

For over 15 years, Kalikamai Youth Society has consistently delivered impactful programs across Nepal, addressing a spectrum of critical challenges. Our initiatives span youth development, healthcare, child protection, women's empowerment, literacy, environmental sustainability, food security, social enterprise development, and crisis response, demonstrating a sustained commitment to positive change.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have made meaningful progress across various sectors. Our journey reflects the transformative power of community-driven action, and today, Kalikamai Youth Society stands as a testament to the impact of collective effort.
