Our Mission

Kalikamai Youth Society empowers disenfranchised communities in Nepal to achieve sustainable development and social justice by implementing collaborative, community-led solutions for positive transformation.

Our Impact

For over 02 years, Kalikamai Youth Society has consistently delivered impactful programs across Nepal, addressing a spectrum of critical challenges. Our initiatives span youth development, healthcare, child protection, women's empowerment, literacy, environmental sustainability, food security, social enterprise development, and crisis response, demonstrating a sustained commitment to positive change.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have made meaningful progress across various sectors. Our journey reflects the transformative power of community-driven action, and today, Kalikamai Youth Society stands as a testament to the impact of collective effort.

Testimonials

Awesome messages for our
organization

Aakash Chaurasiya

Founder and President, Kalikamai Youth Society

"At the core of our organization lies a commitment to social justice and sustainable development. From championing the Anti-Dowry Campaign to fostering Gender Equality, our mission is to dismantle systemic barriers and empower the marginalized. By preserving the rich Madhesi Folk Art and Culture while simultaneously driving modern Legal Awareness and Environmental Stewardship, we are building a bridge between our heritage and a progressive future. We remain dedicated to nurturing the inherent talents of our children and ensuring a safe, drug-free environment for our youth. Together, we are not just launching programs; we are spark-plugging a movement for lasting change."

ABOUT OUR STORY

Bishal Kushwaha

Senior-Secretary

"Our journey began with a simple belief: that a community is only as strong as its most vulnerable members. Whether we are on the ground for a Tree Plantation drive or advocating for Legal Remedies against Women’s Violence, our goal remains the same—dignity for all. We believe that through Sports Competitions and Capacity Building, we can channel the energy of our youth toward a brighter tomorrow. Our work in protecting our cultural identity and fighting social evils like drug abuse is a testament to our collective resilience. Thank you for standing with us as we strive for an equitable and empowered society"
