Drug Awareness Training Program

Empowering the next generation with knowledge, resistance skills, and community advocacy in Kalikamai RM.

The Kalikamai Youth Society (KYS) recently spearheaded a vital Drug Awareness Training Program within the Kalikamai Rural Municipality (RM), specifically targeting the growing vulnerability of local youth to substance abuse. Recognizing that the border regions are often high-risk areas, KYS organized this initiative to empower the next generation with knowledge and resistance skills.

Core Pillars of the Training

  • Education on Health Risks: Participants were given detailed insights into the physical and mental devastation caused by narcotics, including long-term brain damage and social isolation.
  • Identification & Prevention: Youth leaders were trained to identify early signs of addiction in their peers and learned effective intervention strategies to provide support before a crisis occurs.
  • Legal Awareness: The program highlighted the strict legal consequences of drug trafficking and consumption under Nepali law, encouraging a culture of law-abiding citizenship.
  • Community Advocacy: Beyond personal safety, KYS motivated attendees to act as "Anti-Drug Ambassadors" within their respective wards, spreading the message that a drug-free society is essential for the prosperity of Kalikamai.

By fostering a strong support network, the Kalikamai Youth Society has turned the local youth into a frontline defense against drug abuse, ensuring that the community remains a safe, healthy environment for students to thrive.
