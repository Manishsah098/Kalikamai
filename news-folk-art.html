Madhesi Folk Art and Cultural Preservation Program

Celebrating and sustaining the rich Mithila heritage and Madhesi cultural identity.

[IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Madhesi Folk Art Photo]

The Madhesi Folk Art and Cultural Preservation Program (मधेसी लोक, कला र सांस्कृतिक संरक्षण कार्यक्रम) is a flagship initiative by KYS to promote and safeguard the unique artistic traditions of the Terai region. Specifically focusing on Mithila art and folk performances, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and modern recognition.

Key Program Components

  • Mithila Art Workshops: Training local women and youth in the intricate styles of Mithila painting, providing them with both creative fulfillment and potential income streams.
  • Cultural Festivals: Organizing and participating in traditional festivals, dances, and musical performances to keep our regional identity alive for future generations.
  • Documentation: Recording and preserving rare folk songs, stories, and rituals that define the cultural tapestry of Kalikamai and Parasa.
  • Promoting Artisans: Connecting local artists with broader markets and exhibitions to ensure their work is valued and sustained economically.

Our culture is our strength. By preserving these arts, we are not just saving paintings or songs; we are maintaining the spirit of our community. Join us in celebrating the vibrant heritage of the Madhes.
