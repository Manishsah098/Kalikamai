Distributing the Offerings of Gods

Fostering spiritual harmony and community sharing through the door-to-door distribution of blessed prasad.

Kalikamai Youth Society (KYS) believes in the power of spiritual harmony and community sharing. Following the conclusion of major local religious festivals, our volunteers took the initiative to ensure that the "offerings of the gods" (Prasad) reached every household, especially the elderly and those unable to attend the festivities in person.

A Tradition of Sharing and Inclusion

Our team went door-to-door, distributing blessed sweets, fruits, and traditional offerings to more than 300 families across Kalikamai Rural Municipality. This simple yet profound act of sharing is a cornerstone of our cultural identity and reinforces the bonds of brotherhood and mutual respect within our diverse community.

At KYS, we recognize that true service involves not only material support and education but also participating in the shared joys and spiritual milestones of our people. We are honored to play a role in maintaining these beautiful traditions that have defined our society for generations.

By bringing the community together through shared spiritual values, we hope to continue building a more compassionate and inclusive Kalikamai for everyone.
