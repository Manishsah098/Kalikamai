Tree Plantation Program

Combating environmental change and promoting a greener future through reforestation and student stewardship.

Kalikamai Youth Society (KYS) is deeply committed to environmental sustainability and rural reforestation. In our latest initiative, we organized a massive "Tree Plantation Program" across the various wards of Kalikamai Rural Municipality. Our goal is to combat local climate change, improve soil health, and create a greener environment for the future generations of Kalikamai.

Reforestation and Environmental Stewardship

The program was launched with a ceremonial planting by youth leaders and community elders. Over 1,000 saplings of various indigenous species, including fruit-bearing trees and shade-giving plants, were distributed and planted in community spaces, school premises, and along the irrigation canals.

This initiative not only focused on planting but also on "Care and Growth." Each student participant was assigned an "Adopt-a-Tree" responsibility, ensuring the saplings are watered and protected during their critical first few months. KYS believes that by involving the youth in environmental stewardship today, we are planting the seeds of a more resilient and sustainable tomorrow.

We remain dedicated to promoting a culture of conservation and encouraging every household in Kalikamai to contribute toward our shared goal of a healthier, more vibrant ecosystem.
