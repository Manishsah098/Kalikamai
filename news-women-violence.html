Prevention of Violence Against Women and Legal Remedies

Creating a safe, just environment for women through awareness and active legal support.

The Prevention of Violence Against Women and Legal Remedies (महिला हिँसा रोकथाम र कानुनी उपचार) is one of our most critical focus areas. Gender-based violence remains a significant challenge, and KYS is committed to not just raising awareness, but providing direct paths to justice and security for women in the Kalikamai region.

Our Strategic Approach

  • Safe Reporting Spaces: Establishing community-based help desks where women can safely report incidents of violence and receive immediate guidance.
  • Legal Remedy Counseling: Partnering with legal experts to help women navigate the police and judicial system to ensure perpetrators are held accountable.
  • Empowerment Workshops: Training women on their legal rights and self-defense, giving them the tools to recognize and resist abusive patterns.
  • Community Sensitivity: Working with men and local leaders to foster a culture of respect and zero tolerance for violence against women.

Every woman deserves to live a life free from fear. Through these legal and social interventions, Kalikamai Youth Society is working tirelessly to ensure that gender-based violence is replaced with gender-based dignity.
