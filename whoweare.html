Our Purpose & Identity

Kalikamai Youth Society is a non-governmental, non-political, and independent association of youth. It operates under the prevailing laws and policies of the Government of Nepal.

The organization aims to unify youth power and enhance their skills and capacities through various activities. It seeks to lead youth collectively toward social transformation and work for the welfare of society.

This constitution has been prepared in accordance with the Organization Registration Act, 2034 B.S., reflecting our commitment to transparency and governance.

Our Mission

To conduct awareness programs against social distortions and malpractices, and to build a developed society by uniting the youth community and moving forward together.

Our Vision

A Nepal where every youth has the skills, opportunity, and platform to contribute meaningfully to a just, empowered, and self-reliant society.

Our Approach

Community-led and youth-driven. We coordinate with local bodies to run awareness campaigns and build lasting partnerships — one small step at a time.

What We Stand For
