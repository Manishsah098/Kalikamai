Arts & Culture

Preserving, promoting, and celebrating traditional Madhesi folk art, music, and regional cultural heritage.

A society without its culture loses its identity. At the Kalikamai Youth Society (KYS), we recognize the immense value of our vibrant regional heritage. Our Arts & Culture pillar is dedicated to keeping the rich, traditional Madhesi customs alive and relevant for the younger generations.

Cultural Preservation Efforts

  • Madhesi Folk Art Exhibitions: We regularly host and sponsor local art showcases that highlight distinct regional painting styles, pottery, and textile weaving, providing local artisans a platform to monetize their crafts.
  • Festival Organization: KYS plays an active role in safely organizing major festivals such as Chhath and Holi, ensuring traditional rituals are celebrated communally while avoiding commercial dilution.
  • Traditional Music & Dance: We offer free evening classes to the youth teaching traditional Madhesi musical instruments and regional folk dances, creating a bridge between the elders and the new tech-savvy generation.
  • Language & Literature: Recognizing the fading use of local dialects, we organize poetry symposiums and literature events to encourage youth to write and perform in their native tongue.

By elevating our art and culture, KYS instills a deep sense of pride and belonging among the youth of Kalikamai. This cultural confidence translates directly into stronger community participation and a unified, vibrant society.
