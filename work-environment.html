Livelihood & Environment

Promoting sustainable agriculture, skill development, and environmental conservation to ensure enduring prosperity.

The intersection of economic survival and ecological health is critical in rural Nepal. Through our Livelihood & Environment initiatives, the Kalikamai Youth Society (KYS) is committed to ensuring that local families can earn a living without depleting the natural resources that future generations will depend on.

Core Strategies

  • Tree Plantation & Conservation: We regularly conduct large-scale tree planting drives and community clean-ups to combat deforestation, prevent soil erosion, and protect local biodiversity.
  • Sustainable Agriculture Training: KYS connects local farmers with agricultural experts to introduce organic farming techniques, better water management, and sustainable crop cycles.
  • Vocational Skill Development: We provide training programs in tailoring, computer literacy, and small-business management to help youth and women create alternative, reliable income streams.
  • Waste Management Advocacy: We actively campaign against plastic pollution and educate households on proper waste segregation and the benefits of composting.

By harmonizing economic advancement with ecological responsibility, Kalikamai Youth Society is helping construct a community model where nature and humans thrive side by side, sustainably and securely.
